## Challenge

Most task apps either feel overloaded with options or too limited to support serious personal workflows. I wanted TaskLayer to stay fast, structured, and dependable even when the user is offline.

## Solution

I built the interface in Flutter and pushed task-processing logic into C++ modules so the app could keep a responsive feel while handling reminders, prioritization, and tagging. The product structure keeps notes and todos in one system instead of forcing users to split context across multiple screens.

## Outcome

TaskLayer became a stronger demonstration of how I approach mobile product work: start with a clear workflow, optimize the logic where performance matters, and keep the UI focused on speed and clarity.
