## Challenge

Time utilities seem straightforward until precision, readability, and multiple simultaneous states all have to work together without confusion.

## Solution

I built the app with Flutter for cross-platform delivery and used a high-precision C++ core to support timer logic more reliably. The interface design focused on making timezone data, stopwatch controls, and multi-timer states readable at a glance.

## Outcome

Zz-Tick demonstrates how I handle utility products that need both engineering rigor and UX restraint: accurate under the hood, simple on the surface.
