## Challenge

A scanner utility should feel instant. The challenge was not adding features, but keeping the interaction short, readable, and dependable on mobile.

## Solution

I treated the product as a focused utility rather than a feature-heavy app. That kept the interface simple, made the core workflow obvious, and ensured the time to value stayed low for the user.

## Outcome

QR Code Scanner works well as a portfolio piece because it shows that I can design around a single high-value action and keep the experience disciplined.
