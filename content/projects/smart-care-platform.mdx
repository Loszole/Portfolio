## Challenge

Healthcare products carry more responsibility than typical CRUD apps. The interface has to feel trustworthy while the backend has to keep workflows structured and secure.

## Solution

During my internship I worked across the MERN stack, helping implement secure REST APIs and improving frontend responsiveness so the platform felt more usable on real devices. I also contributed to shaping the UI structure so the product could support richer interactions without becoming hard to maintain.

## Outcome

Smart Care strengthened my full-stack delivery skills and gave me a more concrete understanding of how backend reliability and frontend clarity have to work together in production-oriented software.
