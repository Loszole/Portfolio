## Challenge

Recipe apps depend heavily on reliable local data. The challenge was to keep content organized and persistent while still making the product simple to navigate on mobile.

## Solution

I used Room Database to support dependable local storage and built the interface around clear content grouping rather than deep navigation. That kept the app useful even without network access and made recipe management more predictable.

## Outcome

This internship project gave me stronger experience with local persistence, data modeling, and the practical tradeoffs involved in building offline-friendly mobile applications.
