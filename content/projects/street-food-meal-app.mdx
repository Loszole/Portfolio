## Challenge

Food ordering experiences have to reduce friction quickly. Users want to browse, compare, and order without unnecessary steps or unclear state.

## Solution

I designed the app around fast discovery, favorites, and region-based handling so repeat actions stay simple. The cross-platform structure uses Flutter for delivery while keeping shared logic portable and responsive.

## Outcome

The result was a cleaner food-ordering prototype that highlights my ability to simplify mobile product flows around search, personalization, and action-heavy screens.
