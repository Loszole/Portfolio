## Challenge

Expense logging apps live or die on repetition. If entering data feels heavy, people stop using them quickly.

## Solution

I kept the experience focused on quick entry, readable summaries, and smooth interactions instead of overloading the app with secondary features. The result is a smaller product that demonstrates restraint in scope and clarity in UX.

## Outcome

Expense Tracker shows that even compact apps can be valuable portfolio work when they solve a daily problem cleanly and execute the core interaction well.
