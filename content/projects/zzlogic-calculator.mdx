## Challenge

Advanced calculators often become cluttered quickly. The challenge here was to support more technical operations without turning the product into a confusing wall of controls.

## Solution

I used Flutter to keep the interface responsive and readable while pushing core logic into C and C++ for lightweight native performance. The feature set supports both standard and technical use cases, but the presentation stays structured enough for everyday use.

## Outcome

ZzLogic Calculator became a practical demonstration of blending lower-level computation with a frontend that still feels polished and easy to navigate.
