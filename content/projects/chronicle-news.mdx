## Challenge

News products have to balance speed, clarity, and volume. The core challenge was building a reading experience that stays responsive while surfacing live content in a way users can scan quickly.

## Solution

I structured the app around personalized feeds, stronger content hierarchy, and offline-ready behavior so the product remains useful even on weaker connections. Flutter handled the interface layer while lower-level optimizations helped maintain responsive rendering as more content loaded.

## Outcome

The project became a solid case study in content-heavy mobile UX: live data, tighter hierarchy, and a mobile-first reading flow that feels resilient rather than fragile.
